Introduction

Hi! Welcome to my "About Me" page. You may not know who I am, but you will very soon.

My name is James F. Cole. I am boy as the name suggests. I was born on April 16, 2007 in Paynesville, Liberia, where I spent the great majority of my childhood witth my family.

Education

I began school at a relatively average age - 1yr 3months, and I was enrolled ate the Levi C. Williams School. I was transfered to the Beginners Childcare Center after a year. There I spent two years, during which time I was bumped up a grade because I was allegedly too old for the class. Next, I was enrolled as a K-2 student at the Issac A. David School (K-2 being the last class of pre-school).

I spent ten yearrs at the Issac A. David School, and I would have spent more if the school had not decided to downsize in order to introduce a new system - a decision which has proven very costly to them up to this day. The news came prematurely, and at the end of my 9th grade school year, I found myself in search of a new school. My search led me to discover D-WISE where I spent my high school years.

I graduated from D-WISE in 2024, and I am presently reading Information Technology at the NDUCL
